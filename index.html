01Mandate임무

Mandate

The strategic question this document answers is simple: across the six brands AVO operates or partners on, what products should be built next, and why those? Not a wish list, not a brainstorm, but a research-grounded pipeline.

The premise: US CPG products that have crossed a credibility threshold can often be translated to Korea profitably, if the brand fit is right and the translation accounts for Korean retail and consumer reality. The four-step translation frame (미국에서 된 이유 → 한국 적용 → 디자인 방향 → 채널 전략) is the spine of every product concept below.

This is not a proposal in the formal sense. It's a structured menu, not a contract. Each concept is a place a brand could go, not a commitment that it will.

Methodology · 방법론

Each concept is tested against four questions before it earns a place in the pipeline: why a US analog scaled, what the Korean adaptation looks like, what visual direction reads right for the brand, and which channel rewards the format. If a concept fails any of the four, it doesn't make it in.

02Strategic Frame전략 프레임

Strategic Frame

Every concept in this document was tested against a four-step translation frame. We did not invent this frame for this document. It was already built into the trend research deck in this folder, and applied to AG1 and Olipop as worked examples. We're now applying it systematically across all six brands.

The four steps

1. 미국에서 된 이유 — Why it worked in the US. Not "what is the product," but specifically what cultural, distribution, or product-design factor caused this brand to break out. AG1 won on single-SKU premium positioning that removed choice fatigue. Olipop won on functional-soda framing that converted soda drinkers into wellness customers without a cognitive jump.

2. 한국 적용 — Korean adaptation. What gets reframed for Korean retail. 홈쇼핑 needs simplification (한 번에 하나의 약속). 쿠팡 needs price ladder. 마켓컬리 needs premium story. Convenience store needs single-serve viral. Each US win has to be re-engineered for the Korean channel that fits it.

3. 디자인 방향 — Design direction. The visual translation. AG1's clinical-but-premium typography becomes 어스톤 + 과학적 타이포 + 성분 일러스트. Olipop's playful-functional becomes 단일 컬러 강한 KV + 기능 라벨. The brand's existing identity (Bobsnu's heritage, Pheeew's editorial, Daoom's traditional) determines which adaptation reads right.

4. 채널 전략 — Channel strategy. Where does this product live first. 홈쇼핑 explosion or 쿠팡 ladder or 마켓컬리 premium or CU/GS25 viral or 자사몰 DTC. Each concept names its primary channel and why.

Reference Matrix · 참고 매트릭스

Existing trend research deck applies a 2x2 positioning matrix: Snack-like (Fun & Indulgent) ↔ Med-like (Clinical & Therapeutic) on one axis, and Premium/Niche (Condition-specific) ↔ Mass/General Wellness on the other. Each brand has a natural quadrant, and each concept below is also tagged by quadrant.

03Korean Market Context한국 시장 맥락

Korean Market Context (2026)

Before designing concepts, the question is what's actually moving in Korean wellness right now. Below is the operative landscape as of April 2026, drawn from market reports, retail data, and observation of the major channels.

Categories driving growth

유산균 / 프로바이오틱스. 락토핏 (Chong Kun Dang) still dominates the mass segment, with reported annual sales over ₩4,000억 (~$300M) in 2024 according to industry reports. The premium prebiotic and synbiotic segment is fragmenting, which is exactly where Pheeew and Sensitive Ones can play.

이너뷰티 (Inner Beauty). Korean inner beauty is now a ₩1조+ market according to Korea Health Functional Food Association data. Collagen, ceramide, biotin, and vitamin-skin axis products dominate. CJ제일제당, 농심, 코웨이 all entered, signaling category maturity.

장건강 (Gut Health). Gut health crossed into mainstream conversation post-COVID. Search volume on 장건강, 식이섬유, 프리바이오틱스 has roughly doubled 2022 to 2025 per Naver DataLab. This is Pheeew's home.

저당 / 제로 / 무설탕. Sugar reduction is now a baseline expectation. Zero-calorie soda category exploded post-2022. 저당 candy and gummy growing rapidly.

식이섬유 / 포만감 (Fiber / Satiety). The GLP-1 era. Wegovy launched in Korea Q4 2024, Mounjaro followed in 2025. While prescription is constrained, the cultural awareness of GLP-1 has created appetite for fiber-rich satiety products as alternative or adjacent.

수면 / 스트레스 (Sleep / Stress). Magnesium glycinate, L-theanine, melatonin (Korean dosing 0.3mg via OTC) are growing. Younger Korean consumers actively self-medicate sleep through supplements rather than prescription.

Channels and what each rewards

홈쇼핑. Still ₩6조+ industry. Rewards simple 한 줄 약속, 정기배송 lock-in, single hero SKU. Punishes complex bundles, weak demos, abstract benefits. Best for Bobsnu, Daoom, 대학두유.

쿠팡. Dominant e-commerce, ₩40조+ GMV. Rewards price ladder, fast logistics, top-funnel discovery. Punishes low margin, brand erosion. Best for Pheeew, AVO, mid-priced supplement.

마켓컬리. Premium grocery. Rewards premium story, clean ingredients, photogenic. Punishes anything that looks supplement-shop. Best for Pheeew RTD, AVO, premium 대학두유.

편의점 (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven, 이마트24). Grew supplement and wellness shelf 2024 to 2026. Rewards viral single-serve, single-shot pricing (₩2,000–4,000), MZ-friendly. Punishes full-bottle supplement, high price. Best for Pheeew shot/stick, AVO single-serve, viral SKUs.

네이버 쇼핑 / 자사몰. Important for premium brands and DTC. Rewards storytelling, subscription, repeat purchase. Best for Sensitive Ones, AVO, premium SKUs.

TikTok Shop Korea. Launched in Korea late 2025. Still scaling but rewarding viral product-form-factor combos similar to US TikTok Shop. Bloom Nutrition's US playbook is very directly applicable. Best for Pheeew, AVO, anything visual or single-serve.

06.1Bobsnu밥스누

Bobsnu · 서울대 밥스누

약콩 phytochemical · 서울대 이기원 교수 연구 · CEO 김형서 · 가족 경영
Bobsnu's moat is the 서울대 이기원 교수 phytochemical research and the heritage of 약콩 (서목태) as a Korean superfood. Existing SKUs: 약콩차, 약콩샴푸, 생식, 두유. Current 홈쇼핑 anchor (파이토100, 파이토블랙약콩샴푸). Brand can carry phytochemical-based supplement, hair, body, and beverage extensions, but should not stray from the 약콩 / 검은 superfood spine.
Concept 01

약콩 Hair & Inside Care · 통합 모발 영양제

Bobsnu Hair Vitality Capsule + Drink Hybrid
US Precedent
Nutrafol (~$200M+ revenue, Unilever-acquired 2022 majority stake). Vegamour. Olly Hair Boost. Hair-and-skin supplements have grown into a $1B+ US category, driven partially by GLP-1 hair loss adjacent demand.
미국에서 된 이유
Hair loss anxiety crosses age, gender, income. Medical-grade ingredients (saw palmetto, marine collagen, biotin) packaged as daily wellness rather than treatment. Subscription-native, condition-specific.
한국 적용
Korea has 탈모 anxiety culture and existing 모발 영양 supplement category, but 약콩 has not been used as the hero ingredient despite traditional reputation. Combines existing Bobsnu 약콩샴푸 product line with internal supplement.
디자인 방향
Heritage 약콩 photography + clinical typography + 이기원 교수 신뢰 마크. 캡슐 + 드링크 hybrid 정기배송 box. Black-and-cream palette consistent with existing Bobsnu identity.
채널
홈쇼핑 1차 (탈모 케어 단일 약속). 자사몰 정기배송. 쿠팡 가격 ladder.
차별점
Korean manufacturing partner + 서울대 이기원 교수 연구 + 약콩 헤리티지의 합. 단일 비타민이 아닌 inside+outside 통합.
Concept 02

Bobsnu Phyto Daily · 매일 마시는 phytochemical

파이토 데일리 RTD · 검은 슈퍼푸드 음료
US Precedent
MUD\WTR (mushroom adaptogen daily, ~$30M+ revenue 2024). Four Sigmatic. Athletic Greens RTD line. Daily ritual functional drink as coffee alternative.
미국에서 된 이유
Wellness consumers want a daily functional ritual that is not coffee. Adaptogen framing converts both health-conscious and burnout audiences. Premium pricing on a daily SKU.
한국 적용
Korea has 한방차, 검은콩차 heritage but no premium daily ritual product. 약콩 + 검은참깨 + 흑미 + 한방 mild adaptogen. Single-serve stick or RTD bottle.
디자인 방향
검은 / 갈색 무광 패키지. 약콩 일러스트. 한자 + 한글 typography. Premium serif. Daily ritual photography.
채널
마켓컬리 (premium morning ritual). 자사몰. 쿠팡. 편의점 (single-serve stick variant).
차별점
기존 Bobsnu 약콩차의 premium ritualized 진화. 미국 MUD\WTR이 가지지 못한 한국 헤리티지 깊이.
Concept 03

Bobsnu Phyto Trio · 검은 슈퍼푸드 분말 스틱

검은 phytochemical 3종 (약콩 + 검은참깨 + 흑미) 스틱
US Precedent
Bloom Nutrition Greens ($100M+ ARR, TikTok Shop dominance 2024). Grüns. AG1. Daily greens stick as TikTok-viral category.
미국에서 된 이유
Stick-format daily greens converted skeptics through social proof and visible-effect storytelling on TikTok. Bloom proved condition-specific (gut, debloat, energy) variants drive subscription.
한국 적용
Korea has 미숫가루, 선식 traditional but no modern premium stick format that owns the category. 약콩-led "검은 슈퍼푸드 트리오" with phytochemical positioning.
디자인 방향
검은 무광 + 골드 디테일. 단일 스틱 강한 KV. 영양 인포그래픽. Premium science feel mixed with heritage authority.
채널
홈쇼핑 정기배송 (한 줄 약속: 매일 검은 슈퍼푸드). TikTok Shop Korea (viral). 자사몰 정기.
차별점
미국 Bloom의 "녹색" 대신 한국이 신뢰하는 "검은색" superfood. 단일 SKU 프리미엄 단순화.
06.2Daoom다움

다움 · Daoom

1988년 창립 · 38년 가업 · 김수경 박사 · 한국 최초 생식 발명 · 3대 가업
Daoom's moat is heritage. 38 years, 김수경 박사, the inventor of 생식 in Korea. The brand has natural authority that no startup can buy. Product range can extend from 생식 into raw-ingredient-based supplement powders, premium 한방 adaptogens, and traditional-meets-modern wellness, but should never stray from the "healing through food" thesis.
Concept 01

Daoom Daily 생식 · 38년 헤리티지 단일 스틱

Daoom Modern 생식 Daily Stick · AG1의 한국 답
US Precedent
AG1 ($1.2B valuation, 2024 Forbes). Single-SKU daily greens, premium subscription. The AG1 playbook: one product, one promise, premium price, lifetime customer.
미국에서 된 이유
단일 SKU + premium positioning removes choice fatigue. "If I do one thing for my health, this is it." Subscription locks in lifetime value at high CAC.
한국 적용
한국에는 미숫가루, 선식 전통이 있지만 현대화된 premium daily 생식 product가 없다. Daoom의 38년 헤리티지가 정확히 이 자리에 들어맞는다. AG1 like single-SKU, single-benefit, single-stick.
디자인 방향
Modern 한지 텍스처 + 김수경 박사 sign + science-backed nutritional infographic. 38년 헤리티지 mark prominently. Earth-tone palette.
채널
홈쇼핑 1차 (38년 가업 + 한국 최초 생식 스토리 강력). 마켓컬리. 자사몰 정기배송.
차별점
한국 최초 생식 발명자가 만든 modern daily 생식. 미국 AG1이 흉내낼 수 없는 진짜 헤리티지.
Concept 02

Daoom 한방 Adaptogen Powder · 한방 어댑토겐 분말

홍삼·황기·당귀 어댑토겐 데일리 파우더
US Precedent
Sun Potion, Anima Mundi, Moon Juice (premium adaptogen powder, $40-80 per jar). Niche but high-LTV.
미국에서 된 이유
Adaptogen framing for stress and burnout. Premium pricing on traditional Eastern ingredients repackaged for Western wellness audience. Subscription friendly.
한국 적용
한국에는 한방 정통이 있지만 모던 패키징 + daily ritual format으로 정리된 product가 부족. Daoom의 healing-through-food 신념과 직결.
디자인 방향
한지 + 묵 + 약재 일러스트. 38년 헤리티지 + 김수경 박사 추천 도장. Premium glass jar OR refillable pouch.
채널
마켓컬리 (premium). 자사몰. 마켓컬리 → 쿠팡 ladder.
차별점
미국 어댑토겐 브랜드가 한국 한방을 흉내내지만, Daoom은 한국에서 38년 한방 진짜를 한다.
Concept 03

Daoom 식사 대용 RTD · 생식 마시는 식사

Daoom Drinkable Meal · 생식 RTD 한 끼
US Precedent
Huel RTD (~$200M+ revenue 2024 reported). Soylent. Kachava. Drinkable meal-replacement category.
미국에서 된 이유
Time-poor wellness consumers want a complete meal in a bottle. Plant-based, low-sugar, nutrient-complete. Office worker daily lunch replacement.
한국 적용
한국에서 식사 대용 카테고리는 빠르게 성장 중 (단백질 RTD, 제로 칼로리 RTD). 다움의 생식 헤리티지가 가장 자연스러운 진입점.
디자인 방향
Modern bottle + heritage label. 영양 정보 강한 디자인. 직장인 daily lunch 컨텍스트.
채널
편의점 (직장인 점심). 마켓컬리. 쿠팡.
차별점
서양식 meal-replacement이 아닌 "한국 생식의 마실 수 있는 진화."
06.3대학두유Daehak Soymilk

대학두유

정선·평창 콩 농가 공동 소유 · 서울대 평창 캠퍼스 제조 · 농가 equity 모델
대학두유's moat is the farmer-equity story and Pyeongchang clean-region credibility. Soymilk core. Brand should stay heavily focused on soymilk and soy-based products: functional soy beverages, soy protein, fermented soy, and barista-grade soymilk. Should not lose the agricultural integrity story or stray from soy.
Concept 01

대학두유 Functional Soy RTD · 기능성 두유 음료

단백질 + 프로바이오틱 + 식이섬유 기능성 두유
US Precedent
Owyn (plant protein RTD, acquired by PepsiCo 2024). Koia. Ripple. Plant-protein RTD has scaled from niche to mainstream in US.
미국에서 된 이유
Vegan-clean, no whey, allergen-friendly. Convenience-store-ready single-serve. Functional add-ons (caffeine, probiotic, collagen alt) extended SKU range.
한국 적용
한국 두유 시장은 매일유업, 정식품 dominant이지만 functional + premium 카테고리는 비어 있다. 농가 공동 소유 + Pyeongchang 평창 청정지역 story가 차별점.
디자인 방향
농가 사진 + 평창 산 일러스트. 단백질 / 식이섬유 / 프로바이오틱 라벨 강력. Modern but rooted.
채널
편의점 1차 (직장인 단백질 음료). 마켓컬리. 쿠팡.
차별점
매일유업/정식품 대량생산 vs 농가 공동 소유 + 평창 제조의 신뢰 story.
Concept 02

대학두유 Drinkable Soy Probiotic · 발효 두유 RTD

발효 두유 + 식물성 프로바이오틱 + 단백질 마실 수 있는 요거트
US Precedent
Forager Project (cashew-based drinkable yogurt, $50M+ reported revenue). Bored Cow. Lifeway Kefir. Plant-based drinkable probiotic category $500M+ in US.
미국에서 된 이유
Lactose-intolerant + plant-curious consumers want gut benefit without dairy. Drinkable yogurt format wins on convenience. Probiotic claim translates premium pricing without requiring supplement identity.
한국 적용
한국 발효 두유 (콩물, 쉰두유) 전통이 있지만 modern drinkable probiotic 형태로 packaged된 brand가 부재. 대학두유의 평창 콩 + 발효 R&D 결합 가능. 매일유업 식물성 라인의 농가 답.
디자인 방향
농가 + 발효 항아리 일러스트. CFU 수치 + 식물성 단백질 라벨 강력. Modern but rooted bottle.
채널
